As part of [Operation Endgame S03E03 - S03E04](https://www.operation-endgame.com/) law enforcement has seized victim data from StealC command and control servers.

We have received the first datasets from the Dutch Police containing credentions of over 27M credentials.

We encourage certs, csirts and security teams to look at the extracts we made from this information and request information about domains in ther constituency. See the [our instructions for organissation regarding case DIVD-2026-00007](/DIVD-2026-00007/organisation-instructions/) for more information and how you can check if your organisation/constituency has been affected.
